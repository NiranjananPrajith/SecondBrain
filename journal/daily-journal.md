# Daily Journal

Track your day here. Start each morning by asking "What should I work on today?" — I'll review the relevant files and give you an overview.

---

## Format

**This is your living daily record.** Write freely. I'll organize it.

### Sections (use as needed)

- **Morning Brief** — what to work on today, pulled from plans and priorities
- **Updates** — progress as it happens (I'll update parent documents automatically)
- **Thoughts** — reflections, observations, anything worth noting
- **Questions** — things you want to investigate or decide later
- **End-of-day summary** — what got done, what didn't, what carries forward
