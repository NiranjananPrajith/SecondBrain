# CuratorHub Network

*The Social Marketplace for Creators*

---

## Overview

A platform for sharing, discovering, and monetizing creative work — from stories and art to applications.

**Domain:** curatorhub.net (future)

---

## Core Features

### Social
- Share creations publicly or privately
- Follow creators
- Comments and feedback
- Communities/groups

### Discovery
- Browse by category
- Search across all media types
- Curated collections
- Trending content

### Monetization
- Subscription tiers (like Patreon)
- One-time purchases
- Tips/donations
- Premium content

---

## Platform Integration

Network connects to Studio and Dev:

| Source | Content Shared |
|--------|---------------|
| **Studio** | Stories, comics, visual novels, art |
| **Dev** | Applications, tools, templates |

---

## Business Model

| Revenue Stream | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Platform fee** | % of sales/subscriptions |
| **Premium features** | Enhanced visibility, analytics |
| **Subscriptions** | Creator subscriptions |
