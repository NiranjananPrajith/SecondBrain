# Card Submission Guidelines

> [!todo]
> This is a living document. Add points as edge cases arise.

## Core Principle

RVR Chat is a platform for **AI roleplay companionship** — not a vehicle for religious, political, or social activism. Character cards should entertain and engage, not provoke or preach.

---

## What Is NOT Permitted in Public Card Submissions

### Religious Content — ZERO TOLERANCE

- **No real deities or prophets** — Krishna, Shiva, Rama, Allah, Jesus, etc.
- **No mythological figures in actively worshipped contexts** — if millions of people pray to it, it's off-limits
- **No religious symbols as primary character elements** — trishul, om, cross, etc.
- **No religious clergy or spiritual leader archetypes** — monks, priests, gurus in their official roles
- **Why:** India is religiously volatile. Even well-intentioned depictions can trigger backlash, accusations of blasphemy, or community outrage. This is not worth the risk.

**Exception:** Folkloric beings and extinct-mythology figures (Yaksha, Apsara, Gandharva) may be acceptable — these are not actively worshipped. Use judgment.

### Political Content

- No real politicians or political figures (Modi, Rahul Gandhi, Trump, etc.)
- No real political parties or movements
- No depictions of real political events
- Fictional politicians are fine (e.g., "Corrupt MP Amarthya" is a Bollywood archetype, not a real person)

### Sectarian / Caste Content

- No caste-based characters or storylines
- No sectarian slurs or casteist language
- No "lower caste hero" or "upper caste villain" tropes
- India has enough caste tension — don't amplify it on our platform

### Real Persons

- No real living individuals
- No real deceased persons without heirs' permission
- No celebrity likenesses
- Fictional characters inspired by archetypes are fine (e.g., "Bollywood action hero" is fine; "Hrithik Roshan" is not)

### Explicit / NSFW Content

- No sexual content in public cards
- No graphic violence descriptions
- No "romance" mechanics that involve minors
- Keep first messages and descriptions SFW — private chats handle the rest

### Hate Speech & Discriminatory Content

- No characters built around hatred of any group (religion, caste, gender, sexuality, ethnicity)
- No slur-based humor or discriminatory language in character voice
- Characters can be villains with bigoted traits (e.g., a "corrupt politician" can be casteist in backstory) but the character card itself should not celebrate or normalize discrimination

---

## Character Card Format Requirements

Every submitted card must include:

1. **NAME** — Original fictional name. No real names.
2. **BACKSTORY** — 3-5 sentences. Sets context without preaching.
3. **PERSONALITY** — 5 traits. Written in third person, culturally grounded.
4. **SPEECH PATTERN** — Describe how they speak (Hinglish flavor, language mix, quirks).
5. **FIRST MESSAGE** — ONE line of dialogue only. Must be SFW.
6. **TAGS** — Language tags (Hinglish, Tanglish, Manglish, etc.)

---

## Review Criteria (Admin Checklist)

When reviewing community submissions:

- [ ] Does the character name or backstory reference a real deity or religious figure?
- [ ] Does the card depict real politicians, political parties, or real political events?
- [ ] Is the character underage (below 18)?
- [ ] Does the first message contain sexual, violent, or otherwise NSFW content?
- [ ] Does the character exist primarily to promote hate or discrimination against a group?
- [ ] Could this card trigger a religious or cultural backlash if widely shared?
- [ ] Is the speech pattern description accurate and non-offensive?
- [ ] Does the backstory contain elements that would embarrass or harm a real person?

**If ANY check fails — reject with explanation. Do not negotiate.**

## Rejection Response Template

> [!note]
> When rejecting a submission, cite the specific guideline violated. Do not explain at length — just cite and close.

"Your submission was not approved because it violates Guideline [#X — Deity/Religious Content]. RVR does not accept character cards depicting real deities or actively worshipped figures. You may resubmit with a fictional archetype inspired by the same cultural aesthetic."

## What TO Submit — Examples of Good Cards

**Bollywood archetypes** — Fine:
- "Veergati" — Revenge-driven hero wronged by a gangster uncle
- "Sultana" — Femme fatale villainess
- "Majnu" — Best friend comic relief, obsessive romantic

**Mythology-inspired archetypes** — Fine:
- "The Flirtatious Trickster" — inspired by trickster figures in folklore, not a specific deity
- "The Warrior Queen" — battle commander archetype, not Durga specifically

**Original fantasy** — Fine:
- "Yaksha" — folkloric forest spirit (not actively worshipped)
- "Apsara" — celestial dancer from ancient mythology (extinct-mythology context)

**Modern Indian life** — Fine:
- "Arjun" — College student, campus Hinglish
- "Neha" — Office coworker, professional but friendly
