# 🎙️ RVR Chat — What's New and What You're Getting Access To

Hey everyone,

It's been a few weeks since the last proper update on RVR Chat, and honestly, a lot has changed under the hood. I wanted to write this one specifically for the people supporting this — so you know what's live, what's improved, and what you've got early access to before anyone else.

---

## Six Languages, Actually Working

This was the hardest part to get right. Most AI platforms either refuse Romanized Hinglish or respond in Hindi that sounds like it was translated by a committee. RVR Chat now speaks all six code-mixed languages — **Hinglish, Tanglish, Manglish, Kanglish, Benglish, and Tenglish** — natively. It detects what you're writing and responds in the same register, with the slang and rhythm of each language.

If you've tried talking to an AI in Tanglish before, you know how frustrating it is when it defaults to something else. This is fixed now.

---

## Characters That Actually Remember

The lorebook system got a complete rewrite. Before, characters would slowly forget their own backstories after a few exchanges — what people call "context rot." It's the thing that breaks immersion on every other platform.

RVR Chat characters now maintain **deep, persistent memory** across sessions. Their personalities, their relationships, the things they've been through — it all stays consistent. You build a character with a rich backstory, they remember it. Every time.

This matters for the kind of long-form storytelling this platform is built for.

---

## Voice AI Is Live — With Bonus Credits for Patrons

The three-tier voice system is now running:

- **Basic TTS** — 5 credits per generation, expressive Indian-accented voice
- **Advanced TTS** — 10 credits, higher quality output
- **Custom Voice Cloning** — 20 credits, bring your own voice sample

**Patreon supporters get monthly voice credits loaded on the 1st of every month.** Starting this month — every supporter at the Chat Companion tier and above has their allocation waiting. Flame and Inferno supporters get higher allocations depending on their tier.
