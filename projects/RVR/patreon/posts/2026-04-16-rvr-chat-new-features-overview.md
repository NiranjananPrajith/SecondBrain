# 🎙️ RVR Chat — What's New

Hey everyone,

Quick update on what's been built and what's now live for Patreon supporters. Keeping it short.

## Six Languages, Actually Working

Most AI platforms refuse Romanized Hinglish or respond in stilted translated Hindi. RVR Chat now speaks all six code-mixed languages natively — **Hinglish, Tanglish, Manglish, Kanglish, Benglish, and Tenglish** — with the slang and rhythm of each. No lectures, no corrections.

## Characters That Remember

The lorebook system got a full rewrite. Characters now keep their backstories, relationships, and story events across sessions. No more context rot. Build a character once, they stay consistent every time you chat.

## Voice AI Is Live

Three tiers running:

- **Basic TTS** — 5 credits, expressive Indian-accented voice
- **Advanced TTS** — 10 credits, higher quality
- **Custom Voice Cloning** — 20 credits, bring your own sample

**Patreon supporters get monthly voice credits loaded on the 1st.** Flame and Inferno supporters get higher allocations.

## Premium Tiers Are Live

- **Chat Companion ($3)** — 60 messages/day, early feature access
- **Story Architect ($7)** — Unlimited messages, advanced voice tools, priority support
- **Founding Member ($15)** — Everything plus lifetime status, founding badge, direct line to me
