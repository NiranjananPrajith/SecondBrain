# 🎙️ RVR Chat — What's New

Hey everyone,

Quick update on what's been built and what's live. Keeping it short.

---

## Six Languages, Actually Working

Most AI platforms refuse Romanized Hinglish or respond in stilted translated Hindi. RVR Chat now speaks all six code-mixed languages — **Hinglish, Tanglish, Manglish, Kanglish, Benglish, and Tenglish** — with the slang and rhythm of each. No lectures, no corrections.

## Characters That Remember

The RAG-based lorebook system is deployed and functional. Characters keep their backstories, relationships, and story events across sessions. No more context rot. Build a character once, they stay consistent every time you chat.

## Voice AI Is Live

Three tiers running:

- **Basic TTS** — 5 credits, expressive Indian-accented voice
- **Advanced TTS** — 10 credits, higher quality output
- **Custom Voice Cloning** — 20 credits, bring your own voice sample

**Patreon supporters get monthly bonus credits on top of their subscription.** Spark supporters get +500/mo, Flame gets +1,250/mo, and Inferno gets +2,500/mo.

## RVR Chat Subscriptions

Users can subscribe directly via Stripe:

- **Basic** — 1,000 credits/mo for $3
- **Pro** — 2,500 credits/mo for $6
- **Ultra** — 5,000 credits/mo for $9

All tiers include access to the character creator, cloud saves, and premium voice features.
