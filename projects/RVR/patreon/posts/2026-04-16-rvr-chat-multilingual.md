# 🌏 RVR Chat Now Speak Your Local Languages

Hey everyone,

Quick update from the RVR Chat — I have been working on this for a few months now and here are the things I built.

---

## All Characters Can Now Speak Seven Languages

I have added multi-lingual support and all characters can now speak **English, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Telugu, and Bengali**, no matter the language in which the scenario card is created. Now we have full native script support for all seven languages — you can take a scenario card created in English and chat with the characters in Hindi. The character switches seamlessly. No language mixing. No "unsupported language" errors.

---

## Why This Took a While

Getting Romanized code-mixed Indian languages to work properly is genuinely hard. Most AI platforms refuse them outright. Tanglish support is almost nonexistent outside of research projects. I went through a few iterations before landing on something that feels natural — not perfect, but the best I've seen in this space.
