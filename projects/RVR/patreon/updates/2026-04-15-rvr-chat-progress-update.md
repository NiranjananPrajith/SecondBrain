# RVR Chat — What's New for Patreon Supporters

**Hey everyone — big month on RVR Chat. Here's what's been built and what Patreon supporters are getting access to first.**

---

## New: Six Languages, Fully Live

RVR Chat now speaks all six code-mixed languages natively — Hinglish, Tanglish, Manglish, Kanglish, Benglish, and Tenglish. This isn't a translation layer. The platform detects what you're writing and responds in the same register, with the slang and rhythm of each language.

This was the hardest technical piece to get right. Most AI models either refuse Romanized Indic input or respond in stilted, obviously translated Hindi. Getting authentic code-mixing working properly required a different approach — and it's finally solid.

Patreon supporters: **your feedback on language accuracy directly shapes what gets tuned next.**

---

## New: Voice AI Is Live

The three-tier voice system is now active:

- **Basic TTS** — 5 credits per generation, expressive Indian-accented voice
- **Advanced TTS** — 10 credits, higher quality output
- **Custom Voice Cloning** — 20 credits, bring your own voice sample

Patreon supporters get **bonus voice credits each month** as part of their tier. Starting this month, every supporter at the Chat Companion tier and above gets their monthly credit allocation loaded on the 1st.

---

## New: Character Cards That Actually Remember

The lorebook system got a complete rewrite. Characters now maintain deep, persistent memory across sessions — their backstories, relationship dynamics, story events, even the tone of how they speak. The "context rot" that makes other platforms frustrating — where characters forget who they are after a few exchanges — is gone.

You can build characters with detailed personalities, speech patterns, and lore entries, and they stay consistent. Patreon supporters get access to the **advanced lorebook tools** before they hit the public gallery: more fields, deeper context injection, and priority card approval.

---

## New: Premium Tiers and Early Access

Patreon supporters now unlock tiers with real perks:

- **Chat Companion ($3/mo)** — 60 messages/day (vs. 30 free), early access to new features
- **Story Architect ($7/mo)** — Unlimited messages, advanced voice AI, priority support
- **Founding Member ($15/mo)** — Everything plus lifetime subscription, founding member badge, direct dev access

All tiers include the monthly voice credit bonus and early access to the mobile app when it launches.

## What's Coming Next

**Mobile app** — wrapping RVR Chat into iOS and Android via Capacitor. Three-week build estimate. Patreon supporters get beta access before the App Store launch.

**Community character gallery** — 30 seeded character cards at launch (Bollywood archetypes, mythology-inspired, Tanglish anime personas), with a submission system for the community. Patreon supporters get featured placement for their own creations.

**Voice clone sharing** — the ability to share custom voice profiles with the community.
