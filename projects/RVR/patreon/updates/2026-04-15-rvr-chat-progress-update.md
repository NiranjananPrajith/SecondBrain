# RVR Chat — April 2026 Progress Update

**Hey everyone — it's been a month since RVR Chat started marketing, and a lot has happened.** I wanted to write a proper update for the people who have been following along and supporting this. So here's where things stand.

---

## The Problem That Started All This

If you've ever tried to talk to an AI in Hinglish — Romanized Hindi mixed with English, the way most Indians actually text — you know how badly it breaks. Global models either refuse to understand it, lecture you about "proper grammar," or respond in stilted, obviously translated Hindi. The same problem extends to Tanglish, Manglish, Kanglish, Benglish, and Tenglish.

73 million Indians use ChatGPT daily. Most of them code-mix. And almost none of the AI products out there actually meet them where they are.

RVR Chat was built to fix that. Not as a translation layer on top of GPT — as a model that genuinely understands the linguistic landscape of how Indians communicate online. The platform natively detects and responds in Romanized Hinglish, Tanglish, Manglish, Kanglish, Benglish, and Tenglish. No lectures. No filters. No corporate sanitization.

---

## What's Been Built

**The full product is live at [rvr.chat](https://rvr.chat).** Here's what's working:

- **Authentication** — Google OAuth, Discord OAuth, Email, and Patreon OAuth (so Patreon supporters can get access tied to their tier)
- **Stripe payments** — including UPI support for India, which removes a historic monetization blocker for the Indian market
- **AI chat** — AIML API (GPT-3.5-turbo base) with custom character card support and deep lorebook context
- **Character card system** — full lorebook architecture that prevents the "context rot" that drives roleplay enthusiasts off other platforms. Characters remember their backstories, relationships, and story events across sessions
- **Voice AI** — Three-tier TTS: Basic (5 credits), Advanced (10), and Custom Voice Cloning (20 credits). Indian-accented expressive voice, not a robotic Western TTS
- **Freemium model** — 30 free messages/day so people can try before they subscribe. Paid tiers from $3/month
- **Meta Pixel** — installed and tracking for retargeting

The site is a Next.js 14 app, live and functional. Core chat, auth, and payments all working.
